How we Started

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. The company was founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne to develop and sell personal computers.

Since then, Apple has expanded its product line to include consumer electronics, software, and online services. Apple is the world’s biggest company by market capitalization and the largest technology company by revenue.

As of 2023, Apple has 527 retail stores worldwide and employs over 161,000 people. Some of Apple’s most popular products include the iPhone, iPad, Mac, AirPods, and Apple Watch.

The newest iPhone, the iPhone 15

Improved camera

The iPhone 15 features a 48-megapixel camera that delivers much sharper, more vivid, and less noisy photos, especially in poorer lighting conditions. It also has a vastly improved Portrait mode that’s particularly impressive in low light, continuous zoom of video for cinematic framing, and Smart HDR 4 to make your selfies really stunning.

A16 Bionic processor

The iPhone 15 is powered by the A16 Bionic processor, which is a six-core CPU with 2 high-performance cores and 4 high-efficiency ones. It delivers 50% more memory bandwidth than the A15, which should mean smoother performance in graphically intensive games and apps, and an improved image signal processor to deliver the benefits of the new, more powerful camera sensor.

Redesigned screen

The iPhone 15 features a redesigned screen with a new “Dynamic Island” cutout, which is much better for photography and has better stamina.
